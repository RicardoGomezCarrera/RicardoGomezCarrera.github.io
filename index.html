About

I am a PhD Candidate at the Paris School of Economics. My current research evaluates the short- and long-term social and economic impacts of human capital investments across the life cycle, with particular attention to their distributional effects and implications for inclusive economic growth.

I am also a researcher at the World Inequality Lab, where I serve as World Inequality Report Coordinator and was the lead author of the World Inequality Report 2026.

I am passionate about human capital, development, inequality, and labor.

My doctoral research is supervised by Karen Macours, with Luc Behaghel serving on my thesis committee.
