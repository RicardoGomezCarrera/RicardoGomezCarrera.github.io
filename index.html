About

I am a PhD Candidate at the Paris School of Economics. My current research focuses in the evaluation of education and human capital investments, with a focus on their short- and long-term social and economic impacts across the life cycle. My work aims to understand how such investments can reduce inequality and support inclusive economic growth.

I am also a researcher at the World Inequality Lab. I am the World Inequality Report Coordinator and was the lead author of the World Inequality Report 2026.
